Introduction & App Overview

1.1 What This App Does

The Naraendra Farms App is a web-based management system built specifically for broiler breeder poultry operations. It covers the complete lifecycle of a flock — from chick placement through daily production records, egg sales, feed management, electricity tracking, payroll, and final profit & loss reporting.

All four company farms are managed from one central system:

🏠 Kethireddypalli
Rearing Farm (0–19 weeks)
→
🥚 Agraharam
Laying (20+ wks)
🥚 Potlapally
Laying (20+ wks)
🥚 Bodjanampet
Laying (20+ wks)
📌 Flock Lifecycle at a Glance
Birds are placed as chicks at Kethireddypalli (rearing). At around 19 weeks of age they are transferred to one of the three laying farms where they produce Hatching Eggs (HE) and Non-Hatching Eggs (NHE) until the flock is retired (typically 60–65 weeks).

1.2 How to Access

  • URL: Open the app link in any web browser (Chrome recommended)
  • Login: Enter your email address and password provided by the Admin
  • Mobile: Works on phone and tablet — use landscape mode for data entry tables
  • No installation needed — it runs entirely in the browser
⚠ Internet Required
The app requires an active internet connection. All data is stored securely in the cloud. Do not use browser back/forward buttons — always use the app's navigation menu.

User Roles & Permissions

2.1 The Six Roles Explained

🔴
Admin — Administrator
Full access to everything. Creates users, manages all masters, views all reports, enters any data. Typically the company IT owner or top management.
🟣
Management
Read access to all data and full access to all reports. Can view P&L, Company financials, GST, and outstanding reports. Cannot add/edit users or masters.
🔵
Accounts
Manages all financial data: GRN purchases, salary entry, electricity bills, farm expenses, purchase orders, payments, all accounting reports. Also manages masters (parties, ingredients, etc.).
🟠
Site Manager
Manages production data across farms: flock entry, daily records, HE dispatch, NHE sales, medicine, vaccination, egg conversions, hatch batches, feed transfers. Can see employee list but not salary figures.
🟢
Site Incharge
Restricted to their assigned farm only. Enters daily production records, medicine usage, and vaccination data for their site. Cannot see other farms' data.
⚫
Viewer
Read-only access across all modules. Cannot add, edit, or delete any data. Suitable for auditors or silent observers.

Getting Started

3.1 First-Time Setup Sequence (Admin)

Before entering any production data, the Admin must set up the masters in this order:

  1. 1
    Farm / Sites: Go to Masters → Farm/Sites. Add all four farms: Kethireddypalli (type: Rearing), Agraharam, Potlapally, Bodjanampet (type: Laying). Enter electricity meter USC rates.
  2. 2
    Sheds: Masters → Sheds. Add each shed under its farm with shed number and capacity.
  3. 3
    Parties: Masters → Parties. Add all hatcheries (as customers), feed suppliers, medicine suppliers, and other vendors.
  4. 4
    Hatcheries: Masters → Hatcheries. Add hatchery records (linked to parties for egg dispatch).
  5. 5
    Feed Ingredients: Masters → Feed Ingredients. Add all raw materials (maize, soya, wheat bran, etc.).
  6. 6
    Feed Types: Masters → Feed Types. Add feed product types (Broiler Breeder Developer, Layer, etc.).
  7. 7
    Medicines: Masters → Medicines. Add all vaccines and medicines used.
  8. 8
    Vaccination Schedule: Masters → Vaccination Schedule. Add the standard vaccination protocol (week-wise vaccine plan).
  9. 9
    Electricity Meters: Masters → Electricity Meters. Add meter numbers and their USC rates per farm.
  10. 10
    Users: Admin Centre → User Management. Create login accounts for each staff member with the correct role.
  11. 11
    Create First Flock: Flocks → All Flocks → Add New Flock. Enter placement details.
  12. 12
    Assign Sheds: Admin Centre → Flock-Shed Assignment. Link the flock to sheds.
⚠ Important Order
Masters must be completed before data entry. You cannot enter a HE Dispatch without hatcheries set up, or a GRN without feed ingredients defined. Start with masters, then move to data entry.

Formula & Production (Feed Mill page)

Step 1 – Define a formula: Create a recipe specifying what % of each ingredient goes into a feed type (e.g., "Layer Feed: 60% Maize, 25% Soya, 10% Wheat Bran, 5% Premix").

Step 2 – Record production: Enter how many kg/MT of finished feed were produced. The app automatically deducts the ingredient quantities used based on the formula ratio.

💡 Feed Cost in P&L
Feed cost per flock is calculated from the daily entry feed consumption figures × feed cost per kg (derived from GRN purchase prices). Ensure daily entry feed amounts are accurate.

Process Flows

5.1 New Flock Lifecycle — From Placement to P&L

📋 Overview
This flow covers everything from when chicks arrive at the farm to when the flock's final P&L is available. Each step depends on the previous one — do not skip any step.
  1. 1
    Confirm Masters are ready
    Farm, Sheds, Hatchery (chick source), Vaccination Schedule, Medicines — all must be in the master tables before creating the flock.
  2. 2
    Create the Flock — Flocks → All Flocks → + Add New Flock
    Enter: Flock No, Farm, Placement Date, Chick Source (hatchery), Chick Cost (total ₹), Male Count, Female Count, Breed.
  3. 3
    Assign Flock to Sheds — Admin Centre → Flock–Shed Assignment
    Link the flock to each shed it occupies. Enter bird count per shed. This is required for daily entry and HD% calculation.
  4. 4
    Set Egg Opening Stock (if applicable) — Flocks → Egg Opening Stock
    If the flock already has eggs in stock when you start using the app, enter those here. Otherwise skip.
  5. 5
    Start Daily Entry — Flocks → Daily Entry (enter every day, per shed)
    Deaths → Male Deaths, Female Deaths
    Eggs → HE (A/B/C) and NHE (JE/TE/BE/LE)
    Feed → kg consumed
    This data drives HD%, feed cost, mortality tracking, and egg stock.
  6. 6
    Enter HE Dispatch — Flocks → HE Dispatch

Import & Export Reference

7.1 Importing Data

Most data entry pages support bulk import via Excel or CSV. Always follow this process:

  1. 1
    Download the template — Click "Download Template" or "📄 Template" button on the import page. This gives you the exact column structure.
  2. 2
    Fill in your data in Excel. Do NOT change column header names. Do NOT add extra columns.
  3. 3
    Save as .xlsx or .csv (both formats accepted).
  4. 4
    Click Import / Upload on the page and select your file.
  5. 5
    Review the preview — rows in red have errors (e.g., unknown farm name, invalid date). Fix in the file and re-upload.
  6. 6
    Click Confirm Import to save. A success count is shown.
⚠ Common Import Errors
  • Unknown farm: Farm code/name in the file must exactly match the Masters → Farm/Sites record
  • Unknown flock: Flock number must match an existing flock
  • Date format: Use YYYY-MM-DD (e.g., 2025-06-15) or DD/MM/YYYY
  • Numbers: Do not include ₹ symbols or commas in amount columns

Troubleshooting & FAQ

ProblemSolution
I can't see a menu item Your role doesn't have access to it. Contact your Admin to check/change your role.
My data is missing after I entered it Check that you clicked "Save" and got a green success message. If you navigated away without saving, re-enter the data.
HD% shows as 0 or wrong value Check that the shed has birds assigned in Admin Centre → Flock–Shed Assignment. The female bird count must be > 0.
Flock P&L shows no electricity cost Go to Electricity → Allocation and ensure all bills for this flock's period are allocated with % assigned to this flock.
Import fails with "Unknown farm" error The farm_code or farm_name in your Excel must exactly match what's in Masters → Farm/Sites.
I edited a farm/site but old data appeared again Ensure you clicked Save and confirmed. If the issue persists, check that no other user is editing the same record.
Vaccination alerts not showing Check that the Vaccination Schedule master has entries (Masters → Vaccination Schedule). Also check that the flock placement date is correct.
Can't find a party in HE Dispatch dropdown The hatchery must be added in Masters → Hatcheries (not just Parties). Add it there and it will appear.
GST figures seem wrong Ensure GST % is entered correctly on each GRN line. For NHE sales, the rates are pre-mapped. Consult your CA for final filing figures.
App is slow or not loading Check internet connection. Try refreshing the page (F5 / Ctrl+R). If it persists, clear browser cache and try again.
I need to correct a past daily entry Go to Flocks → Daily Entry, filter to the date, find the row, click the pencil (✏) icon to edit.
How do I close/retire a flock? Go to Flocks → All Flocks, open the flock, change Status to "Closed" and enter the close date. The flock will still appear in historical reports.
